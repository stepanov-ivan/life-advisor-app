# Code of Conduct

This project aims to be a respectful, constructive place to collaborate.

## Our standards

Examples of positive behavior:

- giving clear and respectful feedback
- assuming good intent
- focusing criticism on the work, not the person
- welcoming contributors with different experience levels

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment or personal attacks
- discriminatory language
- sustained hostility in issues or pull requests
- publishing private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may moderate discussions, close threads, or block participation when behavior harms the community or the project's ability to collaborate productively.
